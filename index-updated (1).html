About SEEJOB


SeeJob is Division of SolveZone Incoorporation. It is one of the most innovative online job portal in India. The thought of SeeJob came into existence in 2013, and soon it will be with well-known name in the recruitment industry. It connects the people who are seeking the job with recruiters by correctly matching the profile of a jobseeker with the job opening suitable for the one.

Seejob provide the freedom to recruiters as well as candidates to explore accordingly. It provides end-to-end recruitment services to the candidate. As we all know that in the working industry our presence is the first impression to any organization same as well in the online job portal what recruiters search of? They firstly have a look on the resume or the cv which a candidate have given to the HR of an organization so, here we are also concern about the first impression of the candidate who is applying through our job portal. We do create the best in class resume for the fresher as well as the candidate who are working on senior or executive level. We have a dedicated professional expertise team who are working on this, who create the best resume keeping in mind your particular technical skills required for the particular job you are applying which easily gain the attention of the recruiters.


Our Values


MISSION : The aim of our online job portal is to bring a job seeker and a recruiter under the one roof so as to place the best candidate for the job which give them the opportunity to grow their technical as well as other skills in the particular organization. We will continue to do so for years and years. We provide long term success for the jobseeker as well to the recruiters.

OUR VISION: - Our vision is to become India`s largest online job portal website for candidates seeking the job and to be the most preferred choice of recruiters selecting the candidates who are applying through our job portals. To provide world class solutions to job seekers to manage their career potential in an organization.
